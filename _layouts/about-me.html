Automatic Drawing Divination




Art: Example Automatic Drawing



I offer psychic readings through Automatic Drawing Divination. Automatic Drawing is the practice of letting one's subconscious/superconscious mind draw marks on paper without conscious control, and then interpreting the results as a form of spiritual divination. It is similar to a Tarot reading, but instead of using a deck of cards, the card used is drawn on the spot.

Like Tarot, Automatic Drawing can be done for yourself or for other people. In either case, it starts with asking a question or intention. Then it's important to let any thoughts about that question/intention fade away from one‚Äôs mind to allow genuine messages to be channeled through from Spirit into the drawing.

In my own Automatic Drawing practice, simple lines form more complex shapes, and complex shapes fit together to form scenes and a story. This involves an iterative and dynamic dance between the logical/analytical left hemisphere of the brain, and the creative/intuitive right hemisphere. I believe this integration of polarities is a critical task in the evolution of our consciousness, and a powerful way to tap into the Unified Field of Consciousness.

Art & Music




Art: "Temple of Divine Light", 2023



With my artwork I love experimenting with different mediums and ways of incorporating the theme of Unity. One of my favorite symbols/motifs is Sacred Geometry. Studying Sacred Geometry has been a huge catalyst and foundation for my spiritual awakening and understanding. I come from a very logical/atheist background, so the mathematical logic of Sacred Geometry was critical in helping me bridge the gap between atheism and spirituality. Studying Sacred Geometry really unlocked my connection to and trust in Synchronicity as a messenger of universal truth.

One of the primary artistic applications of geometry and synchronicity is with my oracle deck Order of the Twelve Mushrooms. It was inspired by a powerful revelatory synchronicity I experienced in 2017. It involved finding a fairy ring of mushrooms and two trees growing perfectly to represent the stages of life and reincarnation. Upon later analysis I realized the mushroom stages made beautiful perfect symmetries when overlaid onto the Fruit of Life sacred geometry pattern.

Music is another way I love to explore Unity Consciousness. My meditative piano album Temple of Divine Light is based on the 7 Divine Rays of Light, which are universal aspects of creation similar to sacred geometry or chakras, that are found across multiple spiritual traditions. Each track is associated with one ray and its Chohan, or Ascended Master.
